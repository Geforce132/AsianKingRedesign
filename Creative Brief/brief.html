Asian King Buffet website redesign

Creative Brief

Project Background

  • Client: Asian King Buffet
  • Product/Service: Chinese cuisine served buffet style. Business to consumer.
  • SWOT: SWOT
  • Existing Research: Area Demographics Yelp Reviews
  • Date: TBD

Overview

  • Project Type: Website Redesign
  • Reason For Project: We are redesigning Asian King Buffet’s website to offer a more user-friendly interface for the client’s customers via consistency and cohesiveness that this website lacks.
  • Opportunities: Located close to a lot of shopping, local tourist stop, and close proximity to several highways.

Drivers

  • Goals: To increase our brand awareness.
  • Top three objectives:
    1. Increase website traffic by 5% in the next 5 months.
    2. Increase delivery sales by 2% in the next 3 months.
    3. Increase buffet sales by 6% in the next 6 months.
  • Project length: Three months
  • Length of time until goal is achieved: Six months after project completion.

Audience

  • Our target audience
    • Age: Adults aged 25-45 with families
    • Sex: Male and female
    • Race: Caucasian
    • Education: High School Diploma
    • Income level: Median household income $66,772.75
  • What do they think of us? We are the place to go when customers want genuine Chinese food at a fair price.
  • Why should they care about our restaurant? We are affordable and serve family portions. Easy to order or pick up after that commute from work.

Competitors

  • Direct competition:
    • Asian Buffets in Watauga and Arlington
    • Yichiban Buffet
  • Indirect competition:
    • BJ’s Restaurant and Brewhouse
    • Red Robin
    • Logan’s Roadhouse
    • Chick-fil-a
    • McDonalds
    • Carrabba’s Italian Grill
    • Sweet Basil Thai Cuisine
  • What efforts could we duplicate from our competitors? We are an affordable family restaurant which makes us an easy meal solution.
  • What makes us unique from the competition? Our restaurant is based on genuine chinese food made by native Chinese chefs.

Tone

  • What tone should we use to communicate to our target audience? Why?: A lively, amiable tone because we want our guests to feel comfortable every time they come to eat.
  • What adjectives describe the feeling we are trying to project or our approach? Friendly, fast, classic, family-oriented.

Message

  • What message are we trying to convey with this site? We are encouraging new and returning customers to come and enjoy our delicious chinese food. We want the audience to see the website and feel the hunger and desire for our tasty food.
  • Is copyright provided by the client? They are developed.

Visuals

Are we developing new images or picking up existing ones? We will remake the current Asian King Buffet logo from their current website.We can take some photos of the restaurant’s interior and some of their menu items. Any stock images that we need will come from websites that utilize public domain or Creative Commons licensing to avoid any copyright issues. Jaqui will recreate their logo using Illustrator in order to obtain a better resolution image as well as a logo that matches our current audience.
